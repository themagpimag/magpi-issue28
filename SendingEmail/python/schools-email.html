Dear headteacher,

I am writing to you to inform you of the Glasgow Raspberry Pi day at the University of Strathclyde [ http://phys.strath.ac.uk/raspberrypiday/ ]. This event is being run by a group of volunteer IT specialists and experienced lecturers for the benefit of the teaching profession and the general public as a whole. We have chosen the Raspberry Pi for this event due to its versatile nature and potential to develop skills required for industrial and research opportunities.

The Glasgow Raspberry Pi day will take place on the 17th of January 2015, 10am to 4pm, in the Department of Physics, John Anderson Building, 107 Rottenrow East, Glasgow. The aim of the event is to encourage computer programming and associated electronics skills through a series of short talks and an all-day hands-on session in the laboratory. The agenda for the short talks will be finalised during the next two weeks and is expected to include introductory material, classroom studies and industry presentations.

Thanks and best regards,

Dr. W. H. Bell
Event organiser
